About Me

I'm Sahil Sharma, an aspiring game developer currently pursuing my first year of a BCA course at Amity University. This page serves as the foundation of my journey into the world of game development, connecting with my projects and repositories on GitHub.

My passion for gaming stems from legendary titles like GTA, Red Dead Redemption, Prince of Persia, The Last of Us, Watch Dogs, PUBG PC, Valorant, Need for Speed, Call of Duty, Cyberpunk, and more. I'm focused on learning Unity, Unreal Engine, C++, and 3D asset creation to bring my creative ideas to life.
