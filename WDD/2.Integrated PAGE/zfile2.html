How to Apply

Joining Amity University is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to begin your journey with us:

1

Choose Your Program

Browse our extensive catalog of programs and select the one that aligns with your career goals and interests.

2

Submit Application

Complete the online application form with your personal details, academic history, and program preferences.

3

Entrance Test

Prepare for and take the Amity entrance examination or submit scores from recognized national/international tests.

4

Enroll & Begin

Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment process, pay tuition fees, and embark on your educational journey.
