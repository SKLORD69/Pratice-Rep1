About Us

India's Leading Research & Innovation Driven Pvt. University.

The University

At Amity we are passionate about grooming leaders who are not only thorough professionals but also good human beings with values and sanskars.

Our History

Founded in 1995, Amity University has grown from a small institution to a global education group with campuses across India and internationally. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation and commitment to academic excellence.

Academic Excellence

With over 300 programs and 150+ institutions, Amity provides education across all levels and disciplines. Our curriculum is constantly updated to reflect industry needs and global standards.

Global Recognition

Amity is recognized globally for its quality education and has been ranked among the top universities in various national and international rankings. Our graduates are sought after by leading employers worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide education at all levels in all disciplines of modern times and in the futuristic and emerging frontier areas of knowledge, with focus on professional education in a range of disciplines relevant to the current and emerging societal and global requirements.

Our Vision

To establish institutions of excellence in higher education that are responsive to the demand of various segments of society, integrate education with need, develop scholarship and engender inquiring minds. To serve as a catalyst for global harmony that celebrates diversity of all cultures and lifestyles.
