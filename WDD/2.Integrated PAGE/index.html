About Amity University

Amity University is one of India's leading research-driven private universities, established in 1995. With a sprawling campus and state-of-the-art infrastructure, Amity offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines including Management, Engineering, Law, Biotechnology, and many more.

With a vision to be a world-class university, Amity focuses on providing holistic education that combines academic excellence with character building. Our global network spans across multiple countries, providing students with international exposure and opportunities.

Our Key Features

Industry-Relevant Education

Our curriculum is designed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure that students gain practical knowledge and skills required in today's competitive job market.

World-Class Faculty

Learn from distinguished professors and industry professionals who bring their extensive knowledge and experience to the classroom.

Global Opportunities

Benefit from our international partnerships with leading universities and organizations worldwide, offering exchange programs and global internships.

Academic Excellence

Amity University has been consistently ranked among the top universities in India. Our commitment to academic excellence is reflected in our innovative teaching methodologies, cutting-edge research facilities, and the achievements of our students and faculty.

The university offers over 300 programs across 24 campuses worldwide, catering to diverse educational needs and aspirations of students from various backgrounds.
