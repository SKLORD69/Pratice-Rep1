Welcome to Amity University

Amity University is a leading private university in India with a strong focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Established in 2005, Amity has grown to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the country, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across diverse disciplines.

At Amity University, we believe in providing a holistic education that prepares students for the challenges of the global workplace. Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and leadership skills, with a strong emphasis on practical learning and industry exposure.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, world-class faculty, and numerous international collaborations, Amity University offers a truly global learning experience. Our campuses are vibrant communities where students from diverse backgrounds come together to learn, innovate, and grow.
